## Key Questions & Answers

**Q: Does the keepActive option solve keeping the scale size of something when you are moving it?**  
A: Yes (answered by Ashxn)

**Q: How can I add code via the editor (not in world) and have it show up in the app menu?**  
A: Ashxn explained the world folder is an engine/artifact format not meant to be manually changed; users should drag and drop things into the world and let the engine handle storage. Version control features are being discussed but not yet implemented.

**Q: What do you think about pros and cons of folder per app versus scripts/assets/(blueprints or manifests)?**  
A: Ashxn indicated they were thinking the same thing, with blueprints essentially being manifests pointing to models, scripts, and prop assets with metadata and flags.

**Q: What do you mean by 'hittable objects'?**  
A: Objects that can be thrown to hit and destroy other objects, with the app providing them when set down (answered by Agent12)
