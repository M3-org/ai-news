# Hyperfy Platform Overview and Technical Discussions

## Platform Overview
- Hyperfy is an engine/platform powering interactive 3D experiences
- Transitioning from V1 (non-coder's sandbox) to V2 (more code-focused)
- Users access worlds through URLs like play.hyperfy.xyz, fallen.hyperfy.xyz, and tattedalien.club
- Created apps can be shared through storefronts or collections on hyperworld.host
